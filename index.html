Issue Miner

Two separate methods. Core mines Quora and industry forums directly through search. Reddit uses a different path: it opens a Google search, since Google is the only search engine Reddit still allows in, and you paste back what you find.

Use this to rebuild an old post: paste its URL and mine fresh failure points for a rewrite.
